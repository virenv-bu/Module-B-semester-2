Cancer Diagnostic Modeling
This project explores machine-learning approaches to cancer diagnosis by combining tabular
cell-feature data, large-scale patient registries, and histopathological imaging to support tissue
classification, disease detection, and population-level cancer analysis.
Datasets Used
1. Breast Cancer Wisconsin (Diagnostic)
UCI Machine Learning Repository
https://doi.org/10.24432/C5DW2B
2. Cancer Data – Brazil
Kaggle (Brazilian National Cancer Institute registries)
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/joaopedromedeiros/cancer-data-brazil
3. Metastatic Tissue Classification – PatchCamelyon (PCam)
Kaggle
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/andrewmvd/metastatic-tissue-classification-patchcamelyon

(My choice for analysis)
4. Lung and Colon Cancer Histopathological Images (LC25000)
Kaggle
